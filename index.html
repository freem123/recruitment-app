Why Starting Early Matters

Starting early gives you control over your recruiting journey.

Start early so you can:
  • Build a highlight video
  • Connect with coaches
  • Understand your level
  • Balance academics

How to Research Schools

Look For:

  • Roster competition
  • Graduating players
  • Play style
  • Academics
  • Location
Tip: Find the right fit—not just the biggest name.

Contacting Coaches

Email Should Include:

  • Basic info
  • Highlight video
  • Stats
  • Why their program
Keep it simple: Clear + direct wins.

Finding the Right Fit

  • Playing time?
  • Coaching style?
  • Academics?
  • Can you live there?
Balance matters: Soccer + life.

Take Action

  • Research
  • Build film
  • Email coaches
  • Stay organized
You’re in control.
