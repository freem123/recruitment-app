Why Starting Early Matters

The recruiting process can feel overwhelming, but starting early gives you control. Many players wait for opportunities instead of creating them. The earlier you begin, the more time you have to improve, research, and connect with coaches.

Start early so you can:
  • Build a strong highlight video
  • Develop relationships with coaches
  • Understand where you realistically fit
  • Balance soccer and academics effectively

How to Research Schools

Finding the right school goes beyond soccer. You should look at academic programs, campus environment, and team structure.

Key Things to Look For:

  • Team roster (your position & competition)
  • Graduating players (future openings)
  • Team performance and style of play
  • Academic programs that interest you
  • Location and campus environment
Tip: Don’t just aim for the biggest schools—find programs where you can realistically contribute and grow.

How to Contact College Coaches

Coaches won’t always find you—you need to take initiative. Reaching out shows maturity and genuine interest.

What to Include in Your Email:

  • Your name, graduation year, and position
  • Highlight video link
  • Key stats and achievements
  • Why you're interested in their program
Keep it simple: Be clear, direct, and personal. Coaches appreciate players who respect their time.

Finding the Right Fit

The “right” school is not just about the highest level—it’s about where you will thrive. Think long-term about your development both on and off the field.

Ask Yourself:

  • Will I get playing time?
  • Do I like the coaching style?
  • Does this school support my academic goals?
  • Can I see myself here for four years?
Balance matters: Your college experience should support your growth as both a player and a person.

Take Control of Your Recruiting Journey

The recruiting process doesn’t have to feel confusing or stressful. When you break it into steps, it becomes manageable.

Your Action Plan:

  • Start researching schools early
  • Create and update your highlight video
  • Reach out to coaches consistently
  • Track your progress and stay organized
  • Reflect on your goals regularly
Remember: You are in control of your journey. Take action, stay consistent, and trust the process.
